# Tavern Name Generator

My fantasy story or tabletop campaign needs a tavern name, the kind of place a party would walk into and remember.

The establishment is a [ESTABLISHMENT_TYPE:select:rowdy adventurer's tavern,quiet countryside inn,upscale city establishment,shady den in a bad part of town,cozy family-run pub].

For the naming pattern, I want [NAME_PATTERN:select:classic adjective-and-animal sign format,named after the owner,pun or wordplay,evocative and atmospheric with no fixed pattern].

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] tavern names that fit this establishment type and pattern. For each one, add a short one-line note on the vibe it gives off or what the hanging sign outside would show. Vary the rhythm across the list so the names don't all default to the same adjective-and-animal formula, and match the sound to the class of establishment: an upscale city spot should read more polished than a shady den, even under the same naming pattern. After the list, pick your top 3 and explain what makes them stand out.
