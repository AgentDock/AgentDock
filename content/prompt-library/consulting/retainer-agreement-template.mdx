# Retainer Agreement Template

You are an experienced contract and legal document specialist who has drafted hundreds of retainer agreements for consultants, law firms, marketing agencies, IT service providers, design studios, and independent professionals. You understand that a retainer agreement must balance the service provider's need for predictable recurring revenue with the client's need for guaranteed access, clear deliverables, and flexibility. Your agreements are known for being thorough yet readable, protecting both parties while avoiding unnecessary legal complexity that creates friction or confusion.

I need you to draft a professional retainer agreement between a service provider and a client.

The service provider is [PROVIDER_NAME] operating as [PROVIDER_BUSINESS_NAME]. The provider's contact information is:

- Address: [PROVIDER_ADDRESS]
- Email: [PROVIDER_EMAIL]
- Phone: [PROVIDER_PHONE]

The client entering into this retainer agreement is [CLIENT_NAME] at [CLIENT_COMPANY]. The client's address and contact information are:

[CLIENT_ADDRESS]

The type of service being retained is [SERVICE_TYPE:select:Consulting,Legal,Marketing,IT,Design,Financial Advisory,PR,General]. The specific services included under this retainer are:

[SERVICES_INCLUDED]

The monthly retainer fee is [RETAINER_FEE] paid on a [BILLING_CYCLE:select:Monthly,Quarterly,Semi-Annually,Annually] basis. This retainer covers [MONTHLY_HOURS] hours of service per billing period. Any hours beyond the included allocation will be billed at an overage rate of [OVERAGE_RATE] per hour.

The payment terms are [PAYMENT_TERMS:select:Due on Receipt,Net 15,Net 30,Net 45]. The preferred payment method is [PAYMENT_METHOD:select:Bank Transfer,ACH,Check,Credit Card,Wire Transfer,PayPal].

The retainer term begins on [START_DATE] and continues for [TERM_LENGTH:select:3 months,6 months,12 months,24 months,Open-ended with notice period]. The agreement will [RENEWAL_TERMS:select:auto-renew for the same term unless either party gives 30 days notice,auto-renew month-to-month after the initial term,expire at the end of the term unless renewed in writing,auto-renew annually unless either party gives 60 days notice].

Either party may terminate this agreement with [TERMINATION_NOTICE:select:15 days,30 days,60 days,90 days] written notice. Upon termination, the following applies: [TERMINATION_CONDITIONS?]

Regarding unused hours each billing period: [UNUSED_HOURS:select:Unused hours expire at the end of each billing period,Up to 10 hours may roll over to the next period,Up to 25 percent of monthly hours roll over for one period,All unused hours roll over for up to 3 months,Unused hours are forfeited with no credit].

The expected deliverables or reporting for this retainer include:

[DELIVERABLES_AND_REPORTING]

Any specific performance metrics, response time guarantees, or service level expectations are:

[SERVICE_LEVEL_EXPECTATIONS?]

Confidentiality terms for this engagement: [CONFIDENTIALITY:select:Mutual NDA covering all shared information indefinitely,Mutual NDA with a 2-year term after termination,Provider-only NDA protecting client information,No formal confidentiality terms required]. Intellectual property created during the retainer [IP_TERMS:select:belongs to the client upon full payment,belongs to the provider with a license granted to the client,is jointly owned by both parties,belongs to the client for deliverables and to the provider for pre-existing materials and tools].

Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through [DISPUTE_RESOLUTION:select:Binding arbitration,Mediation followed by arbitration,Litigation in the provider's jurisdiction,Negotiation followed by mediation]. This agreement is governed by the laws of [GOVERNING_LAW].

Additional terms, special conditions, or scope limitations to include are:

[ADDITIONAL_TERMS?]

Generate a complete, professionally structured retainer agreement with these sections: Parties and Recitals identifying both parties with full legal names and addresses. Scope of Services listing what is included and excluded, with a process for handling out-of-scope requests. Retainer Fee and Payment covering the fee amount, billing cycle, overage rate, late payment penalties of 1.5 percent per month, and the right to suspend services after 15 days of non-payment. Term and Renewal with the start date, initial term, and renewal mechanism. Hours Allocation and Tracking explaining the monthly allocation, unused hours policy, and time reporting process. Deliverables and Reporting defining recurring outputs and review meetings. Service Levels and Availability documenting response times, priority access, and emergency request handling. Confidentiality with protections matching the selected terms. Intellectual Property defining ownership of work product and pre-existing materials. Termination covering notice periods, prepaid fee handling, and termination for cause. Limitation of Liability capping the provider's exposure at 12 months of retainer fees with exclusions for indirect damages. Dispute Resolution with the selected mechanism and governing law. General Provisions for entire agreement, amendments, assignment, force majeure, notices, severability, and waiver. Signature Blocks for both parties with name, title, company, and date fields.

Write in clear, professional legal language accessible to business professionals. Use numbered sections for easy reference. Avoid overly technical jargon where plain language achieves the same clarity.

This retainer agreement template is for informational and educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Retainer arrangements vary significantly by industry, jurisdiction, and service type. Before using any agreement in a real business situation, consult with a qualified attorney to ensure the document complies with applicable laws and adequately protects your interests.
