# Superhero Name Generator

I need a superhero codename that matches the power set instead of sounding like a random combination of cool-sounding words.

The power type is [POWER_TYPE:select:super strength or physical,elemental control,speed or agility,mind or psychic powers,tech and gadgets,shapeshifting or transformation,no specific power yet].

The tone I'm going for is [TONE:select:bold and heroic,dark and gritty,witty and lighthearted,mysterious and enigmatic].

I want the name in a [NAME_FORMAT:select:single word codename,The prefixed title format,full alter ego name too] format.

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] superhero names that fit the power type, tone, and format I chose. For each one, add a short note on why it works, how it reflects the power, the tone it sets, or the kind of hero it suggests. Vary the naming approach across the list, some literal, some metaphorical, some abstract, so the batch doesn't read like variations on one idea. End with your top 3 picks and why they stand out.
