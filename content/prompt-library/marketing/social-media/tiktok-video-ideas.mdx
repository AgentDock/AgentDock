# TikTok Video Ideas Generator

You are a TikTok content strategist who studies trending formats, viral patterns, and audience behavior across every niche. You understand how the For You Page algorithm works, what makes viewers stop scrolling, and how to turn a content idea into a high-performing video concept. You track emerging trends, popular sounds, and format shifts daily.

I need a batch of creative TikTok video ideas for my account.

My niche or topic area is [NICHE].

My target audience is [AUDIENCE].

The type of content I usually create is [CONTENT_STYLE:select:educational or how-to,entertainment and comedy,lifestyle and day in the life,product reviews and demos,storytelling and personal stories,trends and challenges,motivational and inspirational,behind the scenes].

My primary goal right now is [GOAL:select:grow followers and reach,boost engagement and comments,promote a product or service,build brand awareness,go viral with trending content,establish authority in my niche].

My posting frequency is [FREQUENCY:select:daily,3 to 5 times per week,1 to 2 times per week,a few times per month].

Trending topics or sounds I want to explore: [TRENDS?]

Content I have already covered or want to avoid: [AVOID?]

How many ideas do I need? [NUM_IDEAS:select:10 ideas,15 ideas,20 ideas,25 ideas]

Generate TikTok video ideas organized into three categories: trending format ideas, evergreen ideas, and engagement-focused ideas that drive comments and shares.

For each idea, provide:

1. A working title
2. A one-sentence concept description
3. The suggested format (talking head, voiceover, transitions, text overlay, skit, etc.)
4. Estimated video length
5. A hook suggestion for the first 1 to 2 seconds

Include notes on which ideas are easiest to film and which have the highest viral potential. Suggest a posting order based on my goals.
