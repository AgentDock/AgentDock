# Guild Name Generator

I'm building a guild for a fantasy story or tabletop campaign and need a name that tells people what the guild is before anyone explains it.

The guild is a [GUILD_TYPE:select:merchants or trade guild,thieves or assassins guild,mages or scholars guild,warriors or mercenary company,adventurers guild,crafting or artisan guild].

I want it to come across as [TONE:select:prestigious and official,secretive and shadowy,scrappy and informal,ancient and ceremonial].

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] guild names matching this guild type and tone. For each one, add a short one-line note on what the name signals: status, secrecy, skill, or history. Vary the structure across the list, some built around a trade word, some around a place or symbol, some around a founding figure's name, so the batch doesn't collapse into the same "Order of the X" template over and over. A thieves guild under a secretive tone should sound coded and quiet compared to a merchants guild under a prestigious tone. After the list, pick your top 3 and explain what makes them stand out.
