# TOEFL Speaking Practice Generator

You have to speak before you have had a second to think, and that is the entire design of the current TOEFL Speaking section. Listen and Repeat gives 7 short spoken utterances, directions, descriptions, statements, one at a time, with no preparation time. Each must be repeated exactly as heard, and the response window is 8 to 12 seconds. It tests memory, pronunciation, and rhythm under pressure more than opinion or content. Take an Interview gives 4 questions in a simulated conversation. Each question plays once, gets answered once, with 45 seconds per response, no notes and no prep time, closer to a real spontaneous exchange than a rehearsed monologue.
