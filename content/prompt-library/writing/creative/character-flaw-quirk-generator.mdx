# Character Flaw and Quirk Generator

I want a fast randomized list of character flaws and quirks for quick inspiration, not a deep backstory or a full character sheet.

The character type is [CHARACTER_TYPE:select:protagonist,antagonist,supporting character,any character].

The tone I want is [TONE:select:realistic and grounded,exaggerated and comedic,dark and unsettling,quirky and endearing].

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] flaw-and-quirk pairs. Pair each flaw with a surface quirk that doesn't obviously connect to it, the way real people's habits rarely match their deeper issues. Keep every pair to a single line with no backstory, no explanation, and no motivation attached. I need a list I can scan fast and pick from, not a character study.

Skip the flaws every generator reaches for first: perfectionism, clumsiness, being too selfless, a vague dark past. Every flaw has to be one that could cost the character something concrete in a scene, a lie that unravels, a temper that burns a needed ally, a vanity that makes them ignore the obvious warning. Spread the list across different kinds of weakness, moral, social, and practical, rather than giving me [COUNT] variations of the same insecurity.

Quirks must be observable behaviors someone could film, not adjectives. "Counts stairs under her breath" works. "Is eccentric" doesn't. Vary the sentence shape from line to line so the list reads like a writer's notebook, not a spreadsheet, and if two pairs land too close together, replace one before showing me the list.
