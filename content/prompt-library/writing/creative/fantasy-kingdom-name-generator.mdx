# Fantasy Kingdom Name Generator

I need names for a fantasy kingdom, empire, or nation appearing on a map or in a story I'm building.

The nation is a [NATION_TYPE:select:grand ancient empire,small proud kingdom,contested border area,newly founded nation,fallen or ruined former kingdom].

The tone I want is [TONE:select:regal and powerful,dark and feared,peaceful and prosperous,mysterious and isolated].

Any extra detail worth building the name around: [CONTEXT?] (a founding dynasty, a geographic feature, a historical event)

Generate [COUNT:number:5-15] kingdom names based on my inputs. For each one, add a short note on what it evokes, the scale, the history, the reputation, and why it fits a nation of this type and tone. Give an ancient empire more weight and formality in the name than a newly founded nation unless I said otherwise, and let a fallen kingdom's name carry a hint of what it lost. Close with your top 3 picks and why they stand out.
