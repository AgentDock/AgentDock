This prompt adapts to which situation applies to [STATE] rather than assuming every candidate is studying for the same kind of exam.

Set [STATE] to where you're seeking a notary commission and [QUESTION_COUNT:number:10-30] for how many practice items to generate.

If [STATE] is one of the states that requires a written exam, generate [QUESTION_COUNT] original multiple choice questions on core notarial knowledge, acceptable forms of identification, proper acknowledgment and jurat procedures, notary journal requirements, prohibited practices like giving legal advice, and the specific limits of a notary's authority.
