# Elf Name Generator

I need a batch of elf names for a story, tabletop campaign, or world I'm building.

The elf subtype is [ELF_SUBTYPE:select:high elf,wood elf,dark elf or drow,blood elf,half-elf,generic fantasy elf].

The name should read as [GENDER:select:masculine,feminine,gender-neutral].

The naming style I want is [NAMING_STYLE:select:melodic and flowing,sharp and consonant-heavy,nature-inspired,ancient and formal,short and modern-fantasy].

Any extra context worth building the names around: [CONTEXT?] (a character's role, a specific region or forest, a family lineage, anything relevant)

Generate [COUNT:number:5-25] elf names based on my inputs. For each name, add a short one-line note on what it evokes, a nature root, a formal title, a sound pattern, and why it fits the subtype and style I chose. Vary the length and syllable pattern across the list so the names don't all sound like small edits of each other. Keep dark elf or drow names sharper and harder-edged than high elf or wood elf names unless I asked for something else. After the list, pick your top 3 favorites and explain what makes them stand out.
