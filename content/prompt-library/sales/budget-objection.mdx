# Budget Objection Handling

A prompt to help sales teams effectively respond to budget and pricing concerns.

## The Prompt

```
You are a professional sales representative. A prospect has just said: "Your solution is too expensive for our budget."

Your task is to:
1. Acknowledge their concern with empathy
2. Ask questions to understand their budget constraints
3. Highlight the value and ROI of the solution
4. Offer flexible pricing options if available
5. Create urgency around the cost of inaction

Respond in a professional, helpful tone that builds trust while addressing their budget concerns.
```

## When to Use

- Prospect mentions price as a concern
- During pricing discussions
- When presenting proposals
- Follow-up conversations about cost

## Expected Outcome

- Better understanding of prospect's budget
- Increased focus on value over price
- Higher conversion rates on price objections
- Stronger relationships through empathy
