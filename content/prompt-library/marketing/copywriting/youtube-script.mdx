# YouTube Video Script Writer

I need a YouTube video script that hooks viewers and keeps them watching until the end.

The video topic is [TOPIC].

The type of video is [VIDEO_TYPE:select:educational or tutorial,listicle or ranking,commentary or reaction,vlog or day in the life,product review,story time,comparison,explainer,challenge,interview or conversation].

The target length is [LENGTH:select:short under 5 minutes,medium 5 to 10 minutes,long 10 to 20 minutes,very long 20 plus minutes].

My target audience is [AUDIENCE].

My channel niche is [NICHE].

Key points or information to cover: [KEY_POINTS]

Research or sources to reference: [SOURCES?]

My on-camera style is [STYLE:select:talking head direct to camera,voiceover with visuals,energetic and fast-paced,calm and conversational,educational and clear,entertaining and comedic].

Sponsors or integrations to include: [SPONSORS?]

Call to action goals: [CTA:select:subscribe and bell,comment engagement,watch another video,visit link in description,join membership,all of the above].

Write a complete YouTube script with: a hook in the first 5 to 10 seconds that promises value, an intro that previews what viewers will learn, the main content with clear sections and retention hooks throughout, a strong conclusion, and calls to action placed naturally. Include notes for b-roll suggestions, text overlays, and pacing. Write in a conversational speaking style. Add pattern interrupts every 30 to 60 seconds to maintain attention.
