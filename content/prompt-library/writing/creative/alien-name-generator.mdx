# Alien Name Generator

I need a batch of alien names for a species, a character, or a science fiction world I'm building.

The species reads as [ALIEN_ARCHETYPE:select:advanced diplomat or scholar species,warrior or militaristic species,insectoid or hive-mind species,aquatic species,energy-based or incorporeal being,humanoid but clearly foreign].

I want the sound to lean [NAME_SOUND:select:harsh clicks and consonants,flowing and vowel-heavy,mathematical or code-like,unpronounceable-but-styled for humans].

Any extra detail that should shape the names: [CONTEXT?] (a home planet, a specific role like ambassador or soldier, a first-contact scenario)

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] alien names based on these choices. For each name, note the linguistic logic behind it, why the sounds fit a hive-mind species versus a solitary diplomat, what a code-like naming convention implies about the culture, and how the pattern stays consistent within the species. Keep the sound family the same across the batch so the names read like one language, not a grab bag of different invented tongues. Close with your top 3 picks and what makes each stand out.
