# Travel Checklist Template

I need a pre-trip checklist that covers everything I need to do before I leave so nothing falls through the cracks.

I am traveling to [DESTINATION] and my departure date is [DEPARTURE_DATE].

My trip will last [TRIP_LENGTH:select:a weekend (2-3 days),a short trip (4-5 days),one week,10-14 days,longer than two weeks].

I am traveling [TRAVEL_STYLE:select:domestically by car,domestically by plane,internationally by plane,on a cruise,by train across multiple cities].

My travel party is [TRAVEL_PARTY:select:just me,a couple,a family with young children,a family with teens,a group of friends,a work team].

I have the following health or medical needs: [HEALTH_NEEDS?] (such as prescriptions, mobility aids, allergies, dietary restrictions, or conditions that need documentation)

My home situation while away: [HOME_SITUATION:select:apartment with no pets or plants,house with pets staying home and a pet sitter arranged,house with plants that need watering,home with a security system,rental property I need to secure].

Financial and insurance status: [FINANCE_STATUS?] (such as travel insurance already purchased, credit card travel benefits, bank notifications needed, or foreign currency requirements)

Special circumstances: [SPECIAL_NEEDS?] (such as work equipment to bring, visa applications in progress, connecting flights, rental car booked, or events with dress codes)

Generate a complete pre-trip checklist organized by timeline. Start with tasks to complete four or more weeks before departure, including document verification, visa applications, vaccination appointments, and travel insurance research. Move to two to three weeks before departure for booking confirmations, bank and credit card notifications, prescription refills, and international phone or data plans. Cover one week before departure with home preparation like mail holds, thermostat adjustments, light timers, pet and plant care instructions, and bill payments that fall during the trip. Include a two to three day pre-departure section for packing, device charging, downloading offline maps, printing backup documents, and notifying emergency contacts. Finish with a day-of-departure checklist for last-minute home tasks like checking locks and appliances, confirming check-in details, and verifying carry-on compliance.

For each task, indicate whether it applies to my specific travel style and party. Mark tasks as required or optional based on my situation. Flag any items that depend on my destination country or region. Group related tasks so I can work through them in focused sessions rather than jumping between categories.
