# Plot Twist Generator

I'm partway through a scene or story and want plot twist options that fit what's already happening, not a twist pulled from nowhere.

Here's the situation as it stands: [CURRENT_SITUATION] (what's happened so far, who's involved, where the story is right now)

The genre is [GENRE:select:thriller,fantasy,mystery,romance,horror,science fiction,literary].

The kind of twist I'm after is [TWIST_TYPE:select:betrayal by a trusted character,hidden identity revealed,the goal was wrong all along,a character thought dead returns,the real villain was someone else,time or reality isn't what it seemed].

How hard it should hit: [INTENSITY:select:subtle recontextualization,major reveal,total genre-shifting twist].

Give me 3 to 5 twist options grounded in the specific situation I described, not a generic version of [TWIST_TYPE] that could drop into any story. For each option, lay out the reveal itself, explain how it recontextualizes what's already happened in my scene, and suggest one concrete place earlier in the draft where I could plant foreshadowing for it. Keep the intensity consistent with [INTENSITY] across all the options. A subtle twist should still feel earned without turning the story upside down, a genre-shifting twist should change what kind of story this is entirely.
