This is a curation problem: which picture books actually fit a theme or skill goal, hold a young attention span, and are common enough that a teacher can find them at a library or already owns them, not an exhaustive academic reading list assembled with no eye toward whether the books are realistically gettable.

This is for [AGE_GROUP:select:toddler (2 to 3),preschool (3 to 4),pre-K (4 to 5)], and I want [NUMBER_OF_BOOKS:select:5 books,8 books,10 books].
