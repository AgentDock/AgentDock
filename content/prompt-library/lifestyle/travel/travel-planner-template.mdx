# Travel Planner Template

I need a complete travel planning document that keeps every part of my trip organized in one place.

I am planning a trip to [DESTINATION] for [TRIP_LENGTH:select:a weekend (2-3 days),a short trip (4-5 days),one week,10-14 days,longer than two weeks].

My travel dates are [TRAVEL_DATES] (or flexible within a general timeframe if not yet confirmed).

I am traveling [TRAVEL_PARTY:select:solo,as a couple,with family including kids,with friends,as an organized group].

My total trip budget is approximately [BUDGET_AMOUNT] in [CURRENCY:select:USD,EUR,GBP,CAD,AUD,JPY,other].

My main trip purpose is [TRIP_PURPOSE:select:leisure and relaxation,adventure and exploration,cultural immersion,food and culinary experiences,business with some free time,honeymoon or anniversary,family vacation with kids,backpacking on a budget].

I have already booked or confirmed the following: [EXISTING_BOOKINGS?] (such as flights, hotels, tours, or event tickets).

Things I still need to research or book: [NEEDS_RESEARCH?] (such as transportation, accommodation, travel insurance, visas, or vaccinations).

Special requirements or preferences: [SPECIAL_REQUIREMENTS?] (such as dietary needs, accessibility, pet travel, or specific hotel standards).

Create a master travel planning document with these sections. Start with a pre-trip research checklist covering visa requirements, travel advisories, health precautions, currency exchange, and local customs for my destination. Include a booking tracker for flights, accommodation, ground transportation, tours, and activities with columns for confirmation numbers, dates, costs, and cancellation policies. Build a detailed budget breakdown with categories for transportation, lodging, food, activities, shopping, and an emergency fund, comparing my planned budget against estimated costs. Add a packing checklist tailored to my destination climate and trip purpose. Include a day-by-day overview with key activities, reservations, and transit details for each day. Add a section for important contacts including emergency numbers, embassy info, hotel addresses, and tour operator details. Finish with a post-trip review section for tracking actual spending versus budget, rating experiences, and noting lessons learned for future trips.
