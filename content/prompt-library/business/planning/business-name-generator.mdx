# Business Name Generator

I need creative name ideas for my business that will stand out and be memorable.

My business is [BUSINESS_DESCRIPTION] and serves [TARGET_MARKET].

The industry or category is [INDUSTRY].

I want the name to convey [NAME_FEELING:select:trust and professionalism,creativity and innovation,fun and approachability,luxury and premium quality,simplicity and ease,strength and reliability,warmth and care,modernity and technology].

Style preferences for the name: [NAME_STYLE:select:real words or word combinations,made-up or invented words,name-based like a founder name,descriptive of what we do,abstract or metaphorical,short and punchy,open to any style].

Words or concepts I want to incorporate: [KEYWORDS?]

Names or styles I want to avoid: [AVOID?]

Generate 15-20 unique business name ideas in different styles. For each name, briefly explain why it works for my business. Group them by category like descriptive names, abstract names, compound words, and invented names. Flag any that might have trademark or domain availability challenges. Suggest the .com domain version and note if simpler variations might be taken. Identify your top 3 recommendations and explain why they are the strongest options. Include tips for testing the names with potential customers.
