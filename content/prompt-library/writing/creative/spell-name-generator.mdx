# Spell Name Generator

I'm naming spells for a fantasy story, tabletop campaign, or game and want names that hint at what each one does.

The spell belongs to the school of [SPELL_SCHOOL:select:fire or destruction,healing or restoration,illusion or trickery,necromancy or dark magic,protection or defense,summoning or conjuration].

Its power level is [POWER_LEVEL:select:minor cantrip-level,mid-tier combat spell,legendary or forbidden spell].

For the language feel, I want [NAMING_LANGUAGE_FEEL:select:Latin-inspired incantation,plain descriptive English,ancient or invented arcane language].

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] spell names matching this school, power level, and language feel. For each one, add a short one-line note on the effect the name implies, what a caster would expect to happen when they say it aloud. Vary the length and rhythm across the list so a Latin-inspired batch doesn't collapse into the same three-syllable pattern, and match weight to power level: a legendary or forbidden spell should sound heavier and more dangerous than a minor cantrip, even within the same school. After the list, pick your top 3 and explain what makes them stand out.
