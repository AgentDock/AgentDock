# Lease Renewal Letter

I need a lease renewal letter. The direction of this letter is [DIRECTION:select:landlord offering renewal to tenant,tenant requesting renewal from landlord,property manager offering renewal on behalf of owner,landlord offering renewal with new terms]. The sender is [SENDER_NAME] and the recipient is [RECIPIENT_NAME].

The rental property is located at [PROPERTY_ADDRESS] in [CITY], [STATE] [ZIP_CODE], unit [UNIT_NUMBER?]. The current lease began on [LEASE_START_DATE] and expires on [LEASE_EXPIRATION_DATE]. The current monthly rent is [CURRENT_RENT:number:200-50000] and the security deposit on file is [SECURITY_DEPOSIT?].

For the proposed renewal, the new lease term should be [RENEWAL_TERM:select:month-to-month,6 months,1 year,2 years,custom term specified below]. The proposed monthly rent for the new term is [NEW_RENT:number:200-50000]. The reason for any rent adjustment is [RENT_CHANGE_REASON:select:no change in rent,annual market rate adjustment,property tax increase,completed property upgrades and improvements,cost of living adjustment,below-market correction,reduced rent as tenant retention incentive]. The new lease would begin on [NEW_LEASE_START_DATE].

Updated terms to include in the renewal are [UPDATED_TERMS:select:no changes to existing terms,updated pet policy,new parking arrangements,revised maintenance responsibilities,additional occupant provisions,updated utility payment structure,multiple term changes described below]. If there are specific details for the updated terms, they are [TERM_DETAILS?].

The tenant must respond by [RESPONSE_DEADLINE] to accept or decline this renewal offer. If no response is received, the outcome will be [NO_RESPONSE_OUTCOME:select:lease converts to month-to-month at the new rate,lease expires and tenant must vacate by the expiration date,landlord will follow up before taking further action,lease auto-renews under the proposed terms].

The tone of this letter should be [TONE:select:professional and straightforward,warm and relationship-focused,formal and legally precise,friendly while firm on new terms]. The letter should be delivered via [DELIVERY_METHOD:select:certified mail with return receipt,email with read receipt,hand delivery with signed acknowledgment,first-class mail,email followed by printed copy].

Generate a complete lease renewal letter that opens with a professional greeting and a clear statement that this letter concerns the upcoming lease expiration. Reference the current lease by address, unit if applicable, and expiration date. If the sender is a landlord or property manager, express appreciation for the tenant's tenancy and present the renewal offer with all proposed terms including the new rent amount, lease duration, and any updated clauses. If the sender is a tenant, express interest in continuing the tenancy, reference positive experiences at the property, and formally request renewal under the proposed terms.

State the response deadline prominently and explain what happens if no response is received by that date. Include instructions for how the recipient should confirm acceptance, whether by signing and returning the letter, contacting the sender, or signing a new lease agreement. Provide the sender's full contact information including phone number and email address. Close with a professional sign-off and include a signature block with printed name, title, signature line, and date. Add a note that this letter does not constitute a binding agreement until both parties sign the formal lease renewal or new lease document.
