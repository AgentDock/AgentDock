# Fantasy Town Name Generator

I need names for a fantasy town, village, or settlement appearing on a map or in a story I'm building.

The settlement is a [TOWN_TYPE:select:small farming or trade village,coastal fishing town,mountain mining town,forest settlement,walled market town,frontier outpost].

The cultural feel I want is [CULTURE_FEEL:select:medieval English-inspired,Norse or Nordic,rustic and earthy,exotic or foreign-sounding,invented with no real-world basis].

Any extra detail worth building the name around: [CONTEXT?] (a nearby landmark, a local trade, a founding legend)

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] town names based on my inputs. For each name, add a short note on what it references, a geographic feature, a trade, an old legend, and why it fits a settlement of this size and type.
