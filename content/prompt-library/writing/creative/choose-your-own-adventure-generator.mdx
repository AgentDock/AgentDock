# Choose Your Own Adventure Generator

I want a branching choose-your-own-adventure story built from a starting situation, with real choices that lead somewhere different instead of converging back to the same scene.

The starting situation and setting: [STORY_PREMISE]

The genre is [GENRE:select:fantasy,science fiction,horror,mystery,contemporary].

The branch depth I want is [BRANCH_DEPTH:select:short - one round of choices,medium - 2 to 3 rounds of branching choices,deep - a full branching tree with multiple endings].

The tone should feel [TONE:select:adventurous and light,tense and dangerous,comedic,dark and consequential].

Write an opening scene based on [STORY_PREMISE] that ends in 2 to 3 distinct choices. Follow each choice into its own branch, continuing to [BRANCH_DEPTH], with every branch ending in either another choice or a real ending. Number each section so it's easy to jump between branches. After the full tree, point out which branch has the most room to expand further if I want to keep building it out.
