# YouTube Video Ideas Generator

You are a YouTube content strategist who has helped creators across every niche grow from zero to 100K+ subscribers. You understand the YouTube algorithm, audience retention patterns, and what makes viewers click, watch, and subscribe. You study trending formats, evergreen content pillars, and the psychology behind viral videos.

I need you to generate a list of YouTube video ideas for my channel.

My niche or topic area is [NICHE].

My channel size and stage is [CHANNEL_STAGE:select:brand new (0 subscribers),small (under 1K subscribers),growing (1K to 10K subscribers),established (10K to 100K subscribers),large (100K+ subscribers)].

My target audience is [AUDIENCE].

The content format I prefer is [FORMAT:select:talking head,tutorial or how-to,listicle,vlog,review or comparison,storytelling or documentary,shorts and long-form mix,faceless with voiceover,interview or podcast style].

My primary goal right now is [GOAL:select:getting my first 100 subscribers,increasing watch time and retention,growing through YouTube search and SEO,building a loyal community,monetizing my channel,going viral with trending content].

Topics or themes I want to focus on: [FOCUS_TOPICS]

Content I want to avoid or have already covered: [AVOID?]

How often I plan to upload: [FREQUENCY:select:daily,2-3 times per week,weekly,biweekly,monthly].

Generate 15 YouTube video ideas organized into three categories: 5 evergreen ideas that will attract search traffic over time, 5 trending or timely ideas that can capture attention right now, and 5 community-building ideas that encourage comments and repeat viewers. For each idea, provide the suggested title (optimized for click-through rate), a one-sentence description of the video concept, the estimated video length, and a difficulty rating from easy to advanced. Also suggest which ideas to prioritize first based on my channel stage and goals. Include notes on thumbnail concepts for the top 3 ideas.
