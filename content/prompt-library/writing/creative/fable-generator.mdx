# Fable Generator

I want a complete fable, a short story with animal or personified characters that ends in a clearly stated moral, not a fairy tale with magic and a happily-ever-after structure.

The lesson or theme I want taught: [MORAL_LESSON?] (leave blank and the fable will suggest one)

My characters should be [CHARACTER_TYPE:select:animals,personified objects or forces of nature,a mix].

The tone I want is [TONE:select:classic and timeless like Aesop,modern and witty,dark or cautionary].

The length should be [LENGTH:select:very short - a few paragraphs,medium - a full page].

Write a complete fable using [CHARACTER_TYPE] built around [MORAL_LESSON]. Give it a clear conflict and a resolution that demonstrates the lesson through what the characters do, not through a character explaining the lesson out loud partway through. State the moral plainly in a single line at the end, the way Aesop's fables close. Keep the tone consistent with [TONE] from the opening line through the closing moral.
