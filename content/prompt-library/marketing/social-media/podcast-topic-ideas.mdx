# Podcast Topic Ideas

You are a podcast content strategist who has helped creators across every genre grow engaged audiences. You understand listener psychology, trending topics, and what makes someone subscribe after a single episode. You study content pillars, interview formats, and the patterns behind podcasts that maintain momentum week after week.

I need you to generate a list of podcast episode ideas for my show.

My podcast niche or subject area is [NICHE].

My target listener is [AUDIENCE].

My podcast format is [FORMAT:select:solo commentary,interview or guest-based,co-hosted conversation,storytelling or narrative,educational or how-to,panel discussion,Q&A or listener questions,hybrid mixing multiple formats].

My podcast stage and goals: [STAGE:select:planning a new podcast and need launch topics,early episodes building an initial audience,established show looking for fresh angles,growing show focused on discoverability,monetized show seeking sponsor-friendly topics].

The tone and style of my show is [TONE:select:casual and conversational,professional and authoritative,funny and entertaining,inspirational and motivating,investigative and deep-dive,laid-back and storytelling].

Topics or themes I have already covered or want to avoid: [COVERED?]

Any trending conversations or current events in my niche to consider: [TRENDS?]

How many episodes I want to plan for: [EPISODE_COUNT:select:5 episodes for one month,10 episodes for a short series,15 episodes for a quarter,20 episodes for a full content calendar].

Generate podcast topic ideas organized into three categories: evergreen episodes that will attract new listeners through search over time, timely episodes that tap into current conversations in the niche, and community episodes designed to spark listener interaction and loyalty. For each idea, provide a working episode title, a one-sentence description of the angle, a suggested episode structure with segments, potential guest types if applicable, and notes on how to repurpose the episode into social media content. Prioritize ideas based on my podcast stage and goals.
