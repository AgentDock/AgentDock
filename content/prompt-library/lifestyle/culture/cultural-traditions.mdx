I am most interested in these types of traditions: [TRADITION_FOCUS:select:ceremonies and festivals,daily life rituals and social customs,rites of passage from birth to death,food and dining traditions,spiritual and religious practices,art and storytelling and music traditions,family structure and community roles].

The level of detail I need is [DEPTH:select:a broad overview covering the major traditions,a focused guide on the most important customs,a comprehensive deep dive with historical context and regional variations].

I want to understand these traditions from this time perspective: [TIME_PERSPECTIVE:select:historical roots and origins,how they are practiced today,how they have changed and evolved over generations,both traditional and modern forms side by side].

How I plan to use this information: [INTENDED_USE?] (for example, attending a specific event, writing a paper, preparing a lesson plan, or having informed conversations with someone from this background)

Create a detailed guide to the cultural traditions of [CULTURE_OR_REGION] organized by the areas I selected. Begin with a short foundation section that explains the geography, history, and core values that shaped these traditions. Cover why these practices developed and what they mean to the people who maintain them.

For each tradition you describe, include what happens during the practice, who participates, when and where it takes place, and what objects or symbols are involved. Explain the meaning behind each element rather than just listing actions. Note any rules of etiquette, common mistakes outsiders make, and how to show respect if I am invited to participate or observe.

Address how these traditions differ across regions, generations, and between communities within the same culture. A family in one province or neighborhood may follow different customs than a family elsewhere, so highlight the most meaningful variations. Cover how diaspora communities have adapted their traditions in new countries and what changes have taken root over the past few decades.

If I mentioned a specific tradition, give that topic the most detailed treatment, including step-by-step descriptions of what happens, any preparation required, and the emotional or spiritual significance for participants.

Close with practical guidance based on my stated purpose. If I am traveling, include tips for attending or observing these traditions respectfully. If I am reconnecting with my heritage, suggest ways to incorporate practices into my daily life. If I am researching, recommend primary sources, academic works, documentaries, and community organizations where I can go deeper. Include a short glossary of key terms in the original language with pronunciation guides where helpful.
