# Dragon Name Generator

I need dragon names for fiction, a tabletop campaign, or a world I'm building, and I want each one to sound like it belongs to this specific dragon, not a random fantasy label.

This dragon is [DRAGON_TYPE:select:ancient and wise,young and reckless,evil and destructive,noble protector,elemental type such as fire ice or storm,shapeshifting].

Its size and age put it at [SIZE_SCALE:select:colossal ancient wyrm,mid-size adult dragon,small young dragon or wyrmling].

The naming style should be [NAMING_STYLE:select:harsh and guttural,ancient and regal,short and thunderous,elegant and serpentine].

Generate [COUNT:number:3-15] dragon names that fit the type, size, and style I chose. For each name, add a short etymology-style note on what it evokes, fire, stone, storm, age, or menace, and vary the syllable count and rhythm so the list doesn't read like small edits of the same name. A colossal ancient wyrm should sound heavier and older than a young wyrmling unless I asked for something else. Pick your top 3 at the end and explain why they stand out.
