# Spending Tracker Template

I need a spending tracker I can use on an ongoing basis to log what I spend, see running totals, and know when I am getting close to my limits.

My monthly take-home income is [MONTHLY_INCOME]. I get paid [PAY_SCHEDULE:select:weekly,biweekly,twice a month,monthly,irregular or freelance income].

I want to track my spending on a [TRACKING_PERIOD:select:daily,weekly,biweekly] basis.

The spending categories I want to track are [SPENDING_CATEGORIES] (for example groceries, dining out, coffee and snacks, transportation, entertainment, clothing, subscriptions, household supplies, personal care, or any custom categories that fit my life).

Here are the spending limits I want to set per category for each month: [CATEGORY_LIMITS?] (if you are not sure, ask me to leave this blank and suggest reasonable limits based on my income).

I plan to log my spending using [TRACKING_METHOD:select:a spreadsheet in Google Sheets or Excel,a paper notebook or printable,a notes app on my phone,whatever you recommend].

The main reason I need a tracker is [CURRENT_PAIN_POINT:select:I have no idea where my money goes each month,I overspend in a few specific categories,I want to build awareness before creating a full budget,I have a budget but never stick to it because I lose track mid-month,I need to cut spending and want real-time visibility].

When a category is getting close to its limit I want to be alerted at [ALERT_THRESHOLD:select:50 percent spent,75 percent spent,90 percent spent,both 50 and 90 percent].

If I have money left over in a category at the end of a period I prefer to [ROLLOVER_PREFERENCE:select:roll it into savings,carry the unused amount into the next period,redistribute it across other categories,reset every period and start fresh].

Build me a complete spending tracker system. Design a logging format I can use each day that takes under two minutes to fill in, with columns or fields for the date, amount, category, a short note about the purchase, and a running total for that category. Create a weekly summary view that shows total spent per category versus my limit, the percentage used, and how much remains. Include a monthly rollup that compares my actual spending against my limits across all categories and calculates how much of my income I spent versus saved. Add clear rules for when to trigger a warning based on my alert threshold and a simple end-of-period checklist for reviewing results and adjusting limits for the next cycle. Make the whole system practical enough that I will actually keep using it.
