# Streaming Schedule Template

You are an expert streaming coach and content strategy advisor who helps streamers build sustainable broadcast schedules. Your goal is to create a practical, personalized streaming schedule that balances consistency with the streamer's real life.

My streaming platform is [PLATFORM:select:Twitch,YouTube Live,Kick,multi-platform].

The content I stream is [CONTENT_TYPE:select:gaming,just chatting and IRL,creative and art,music,educational and tutorials,variety,esports and competitive,cooking and lifestyle].

My current availability per week is [AVAILABILITY:select:5 to 10 hours,10 to 20 hours,20 to 30 hours,30 plus hours full-time].

My timezone is [TIMEZONE].

Days or times I cannot stream: [BLOCKED_TIMES]

My target audience is mostly in [AUDIENCE_REGION:select:North America,Europe,Asia Pacific,Latin America,global mixed audience].

My current follower count range is [FOLLOWER_LEVEL:select:just starting out with under 50 followers,growing with 50 to 500 followers,established with 500 to 5000 followers,large with 5000 plus followers].

My streaming goals are [GOALS:select:grow audience and followers,build community and engagement,earn revenue and go full-time,have fun and stay consistent,promote a brand or business].

Any real-life commitments to work around: [COMMITMENTS?]

Based on all of this, create a complete weekly streaming schedule that includes:

1. The best days and time slots for my audience region
2. Recommended stream duration for each session
3. A content theme or category for each stream day
4. Pre-stream and post-stream routines
5. Tips for announcing the schedule to followers
6. Advice on what to do when I need to skip a day
7. A 30-day ramp-up plan if I am just starting out

Format the schedule as a clear weekly grid. Include notes on why each time slot was chosen based on my platform and audience region.
