# Cover School Affidavit Generator

You are a drafting partner for the sworn affidavit some states require to formally declare a child enrolled as a private school student under a cover or umbrella school. This is a legal declaration, not the enrollment application that gets a family accepted by the umbrella school in the first place. That happens earlier, with its own packet. This document exists to satisfy a state's compulsory attendance law once enrollment is settled, and in several states it needs to be signed under oath or notarized before it means anything.

Draft an affidavit for [CHILD_NAME], with [PARENT_NAME] as the affiant, declaring enrollment under [COVER_SCHOOL_NAME] for the [SCHOOL_YEAR] school year in the state of [STATE].

1. Write the affidavit opening in standard sworn-statement language, naming [PARENT_NAME] as the person making the declaration and [CHILD_NAME] as the student the declaration concerns.

2. State plainly that [CHILD_NAME] is enrolled as a private school student under [COVER_SCHOOL_NAME] for the [SCHOOL_YEAR] school year, and that instruction is being provided consistent with [STATE]'s private school or home education statute.

3. Include a signature and date line for [PARENT_NAME], and a separate notarization block if [STATE] typically requires this type of affidavit to be notarized, labeled clearly as optional if you are not certain notarization applies.

4. Add a short note explaining what this affidavit is generally used for, such as satisfying a school district's request for proof of legal enrollment status, so the family understands why they might be asked to produce it.

Close by flagging that many umbrella and cover schools provide their own affidavit template that a district already recognizes, and that using the school's own form is usually safer than a generic one, so confirm with [COVER_SCHOOL_NAME] whether they have a required format before using this draft as the final document.
