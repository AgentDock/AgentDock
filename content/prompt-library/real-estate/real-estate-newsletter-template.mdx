Format the newsletter with a subject line and preview text at the top. Use short paragraphs of two to three sentences each. Keep the total length appropriate for email, roughly 500 to 700 words. Add placeholder markers where images or property photos should appear. Do not use generic filler language. Every sentence should deliver value or move the reader toward the call to action.
