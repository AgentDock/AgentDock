# Home Selling Checklist

You are a licensed real estate advisor with extensive experience guiding homeowners through successful property sales. I need a complete home selling checklist that covers every phase from initial decision to sell through closing and move-out.

The property is a [PROPERTY_TYPE:select:single-family home,townhouse,condominium,duplex,multi-family,manufactured home] located in [LOCATION] with [BEDROOMS:number:1-10] bedrooms and [BATHROOMS:number:1-8] bathrooms. The approximate square footage is [SQUARE_FEET:number:500-15000] and the property was built in [YEAR_BUILT:number:1900-2026]. My selling approach is [SELLING_APPROACH:select:hiring a listing agent,selling FSBO (for sale by owner),using a flat-fee MLS service,working with an iBuyer or instant offer company]. My target listing timeline is [TIMELINE:select:within 2 weeks,within 30 days,within 60 days,within 90 days,flexible timeline]. Current market conditions in my area are [MARKET_CONDITIONS:select:strong seller's market,balanced market,buyer's market,uncertain or shifting market].

My pricing strategy preference is [PRICING_STRATEGY:select:price at market value based on comps,price slightly below market to attract multiple offers,price above market with room to negotiate,need help determining the right approach]. The estimated home value or target asking price is [ASKING_PRICE]. The remaining mortgage balance is approximately [MORTGAGE_BALANCE?] and I need to net at least [MINIMUM_NET?] from the sale.

For preparation scope, my budget for pre-sale improvements is [PREP_BUDGET:select:under $1000 for minor touch-ups,between $1000 and $5000 for moderate improvements,between $5000 and $15000 for significant upgrades,over $15000 for major renovations,no budget for improvements]. The current condition of the home is [CONDITION:select:move-in ready and well maintained,needs cosmetic updates only,needs some repairs and updates,needs significant work]. I plan to [STAGING:select:hire a professional stager,stage it myself with existing furniture,leave it as is,the home will be vacant during showings].

Please generate a detailed home selling checklist organized by phase. Start with pre-listing tasks including getting a comparative market analysis, selecting an agent or preparing for FSBO, ordering a pre-listing inspection if appropriate, addressing repairs, decluttering and deep cleaning, enhancing curb appeal, and gathering all property documents such as the deed, survey, HOA documents, warranties, and utility records.

For the listing and marketing phase, cover professional photography and virtual tours, writing the listing description, setting the list price, listing on the MLS and online platforms, scheduling open houses, creating property flyers, and promoting on social media.

For the showings and offers phase, include showing preparation routines, evaluating and comparing offers, understanding contingencies, negotiating terms and counteroffers, and selecting the best offer based on price, financing strength, and timeline.

For the under-contract phase, cover managing the buyer's inspection and responding to repair requests, completing the seller disclosure form, coordinating the appraisal process, preparing for title search and resolving any issues, and working through all contingency deadlines.

For the closing and move-out phase, include reviewing the settlement statement, attending closing, transferring utilities, canceling homeowners insurance after closing, and completing the final move-out. For each task, note the typical timeline relative to the listing date and flag items that are specific to my [SELLING_APPROACH]. Format the checklist as a printable tracking sheet with checkboxes so I can mark each item complete as I progress through the sale.
