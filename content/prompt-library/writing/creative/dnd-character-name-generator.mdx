# D&D Character Name Generator

I'm building a Dungeons and Dragons character and want a batch of names that match the race and class before I commit to one.

The character's race is [RACE:select:human,elf,dwarf,halfling,half-orc,tiefling,dragonborn,gnome,orc,other or homebrew].

Their class leans toward a [CLASS_FLAVOR:select:warrior or fighter type,spellcaster,rogue or sneaky type,cleric or paladin,bard or social type,no class preference].

I want the naming tone to feel [NAMING_TONE:select:heroic and noble,gritty and rough,quirky and comedic,mysterious,classic fantasy].

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] full names, first name plus an optional surname or epithet where it fits the race and tone, that match the race, class flavor, and tone I picked. For each name, add a short note on why it fits, the race's naming conventions, the class's reputation, or the tone I chose. Keep dwarf and orc names harder-edged than elf or human names unless the tone says otherwise, and don't let every name lean on the same first syllable. Wrap up with your top 3 picks and what makes each one work at the table.
