# Book Title Generator

I need a batch of book title options for a novel or story I'm working on.

Here's the premise: [BOOK_PREMISE] (a short description of the plot, characters, or theme)

The genre is [GENRE:select:fantasy,romance,horror,mystery or thriller,science fiction,literary fiction,young adult,nonfiction or memoir].

The style I want is [TITLE_STYLE:select:short and punchy - one or two words,evocative phrase,a play on words or double meaning,long and literary].

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] title options grounded in the premise I described, not just genre-flavored words strung together. For each title, add a one-line note on why it fits the premise and genre, and flag any that land too close to a well-known existing book so I can rule those out. Mix the structure across the list: some noun-driven, some built around a phrase from the story itself, some leaning harder into [TITLE_STYLE] than others, so the batch gives me real range to choose from. After the list, pick your top 3 and explain what makes them stand out.
