# Travel Budget Template

I need a complete travel budget that accounts for every expense category so I can plan my trip without financial surprises.

My destination is [DESTINATION] and I will be traveling for [TRIP_LENGTH:select:a weekend (2-3 days),a short trip (4-5 days),one week,10-14 days,longer than two weeks].

I am traveling [TRAVEL_PARTY:select:solo,as a couple,with family including kids,with friends,as a group].

My total available budget is approximately [TOTAL_BUDGET] in [CURRENCY:select:USD,EUR,GBP,CAD,AUD,JPY,other].

My travel style is [TRAVEL_STYLE:select:budget - keeping costs as low as possible,moderate - comfortable but not extravagant,comfortable - willing to spend on quality experiences,luxury - premium everything].

Create a travel budget template with these sections. Start with pre-trip expenses covering flights or transportation to the destination, travel insurance, visa or entry fees, vaccinations or health preparations, luggage or gear purchases, and any advance bookings for accommodation or tours. Include estimated costs for each item and a subtotal for pre-trip spending.

Next, build a daily spending breakdown with categories for accommodation per night, meals broken into breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks, local transportation including taxis, public transit, and ride-sharing, activities and entrance fees, shopping and souvenirs, and miscellaneous daily costs like tips and small purchases. Calculate a daily average and multiply by trip length for total daily spending.

Add an emergency and contingency fund section set at 10 to 15 percent of the total budget. Explain what this fund should cover, such as medical emergencies, lost luggage replacement, last-minute itinerary changes, or unexpected price increases.

Include a currency conversion section with the current exchange rate for my destination, tips on where to exchange money at the best rates, advice on credit card foreign transaction fees, and whether to carry cash or use cards at my destination.

Finish with cost-saving strategies specific to my destination and travel style. Cover the best times to book flights and accommodation, free or low-cost activities available at the destination, meal strategies like cooking versus dining out, transportation passes or discount cards, and apps or tools that help track spending on the road. Provide a final summary comparing my available budget against the estimated total cost, showing whether I am over or under budget and where to adjust if needed.
