# Magic Item Name Generator

I need a batch of magic item names for a fantasy campaign, novel, or world I'm building.

The item is a [ITEM_TYPE:select:sword or bladed weapon,staff or wand,ring or amulet,armor or shield,ancient relic or artifact,cursed object].

Its power comes from being [POWER_ORIGIN:select:forged by a legendary smith,blessed by a god or divine power,cursed or corrupted,ancient and forgotten origin,crafted from a slain monster].

Any extra context worth building the names around: [CONTEXT?] (the wielder, the campaign setting, an enemy the item was made to defeat)

Generate [COUNT:number:3-15] magic item names based on these inputs. For each one, add a one-line note on the legend or power the name implies, grounded in the origin I picked rather than generic fantasy flavor text. Mix the naming pattern across the list: some single evocative words, some full epithets like "the Widow's Edge," some named after a person or event, so the batch doesn't read like small edits of the same template. Cursed objects should carry real dread rather than just raw power. After the list, pick your top 3 favorites and explain what makes them stand out.
