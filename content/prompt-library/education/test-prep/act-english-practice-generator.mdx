# ACT English Practice Generator

What's the fastest-paced section on the entire ACT? English, 50 questions in 35 minutes, just 42 seconds a question, faster than every other core section. That pace rewards a student who recognizes the underlying rule instantly over one who has to reason through grammar logic from scratch each time.

Production of Writing, now the largest category alongside Conventions at roughly 38 to 43 percent each, tests topic development, logical paragraph organization, and sentence placement, essentially editing for structure and purpose, not just correctness. Conventions of Standard English, also 38 to 43 percent, tests grammar, punctuation, and sentence construction. Knowledge of Language, the smallest at 18 to 23 percent, tests precise, concise wording and consistent tone, essentially editing for style.

Build [QUESTION_COUNT:number:5-25] questions from [CATEGORY:select:Production of Writing,Knowledge of Language,Conventions of Standard English,Mixed across all three], embedded in a short original passage with underlined portions, the same format the real section uses, where a student picks the answer choice that best replaces each underlined section.
