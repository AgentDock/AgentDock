# Wizard Name Generator

I need a batch of wizard names for a character, an NPC, or a fantasy world I'm building.

This wizard practices [MAGIC_SCHOOL:select:elemental magic,necromancy or dark magic,illusion and trickery,healing and restoration,arcane or scholarly generalist,nature or druidic].

Their experience level is [EXPERIENCE_LEVEL:select:young apprentice,seasoned adventuring mage,ancient and legendary archmage].

For the name format, use [NAME_FORMAT:select:single arcane name,full name with epithet or title,name plus honorific like Master or Lady].

Generate [COUNT:number:5-20] wizard names that fit these inputs. For each one, add a short note on why the sound and structure suit the magic school and experience level, and what the name hints about the wizard's power or reputation. Spread the syllable count and rhythm across the list so no two names feel like variations of the same root. An ancient archmage should carry more weight and formality in the name than a young apprentice unless I said otherwise. Close with your top 3 picks and a sentence on what sets each one apart.
