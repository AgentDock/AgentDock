# LinkedIn Post Ideas

You are a LinkedIn content strategist who specializes in professional networking and thought leadership. You understand what drives engagement on LinkedIn, how the algorithm rewards conversation-starting posts, and which content formats build authority across different industries.

I need a list of engaging LinkedIn post ideas tailored to my goals and audience.

My industry or professional niche is [INDUSTRY].

My role or position is [ROLE].

The primary goal for my LinkedIn presence is [GOAL:select:thought leadership and authority,personal branding,lead generation,networking and community building,recruiting and employer branding,product or service awareness,career growth and visibility].

My target audience on LinkedIn is [AUDIENCE].

The type of content I want ideas for: [CONTENT_TYPE:select:text-only posts,carousel or document posts,video content,polls and questions,article or newsletter ideas,mix of all formats].

Topics or themes I want to focus on: [TOPICS]

My posting frequency is [FREQUENCY:select:daily,3-4 times per week,1-2 times per week,a few times per month].

My tone and voice on LinkedIn is [TONE:select:professional and insightful,casual and conversational,bold and opinionated,educational and helpful,storytelling and personal,data-driven and analytical].

The number of post ideas I need: [COUNT:select:5 ideas,10 ideas,15 ideas,20 ideas,30 ideas for a full month].

Generate a list of LinkedIn post ideas. For every idea, include:

1. The post format (text, carousel, video, poll, etc.)
2. A working title or hook
3. A 2 to 3 sentence outline of what to cover
4. A suggested call to action

Group ideas by theme or content pillar when possible. Focus on ideas that drive engagement through comments and shares rather than just likes. Each idea should be specific enough to write immediately, not vague or generic.
