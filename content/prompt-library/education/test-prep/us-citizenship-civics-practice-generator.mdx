# US Citizenship Civics Test Practice Generator

Which civics test you actually need to study depends on when your N-400 was filed, not on which practice site you happen to land on. Applicants who filed before October 20, 2025 are generally tested on the 2008 civics test, 100 published questions with up to 10 asked in the interview and 6 correct answers needed to pass. Applicants who filed on or after October 20, 2025 are generally tested on the newer 2025 civics test, 128 published questions with 20 asked and 12 correct answers needed to pass. Studying the wrong version wastes real preparation time.
