# Hobby Finder

I want to find a new hobby but do not know what would be a good fit for me.

I am looking for something that is [GOAL:select:creative and artistic,physical and active,intellectual and challenging,relaxing and stress-relieving,social and community-based,productive with tangible results,adventurous and exciting,solo and independent].

I have [TIME:select:15-30 minutes a day,a few hours on weekends,several hours per week,plenty of free time].

My budget for a new hobby is [BUDGET:select:free or very low cost,moderate investment up to $100,willing to invest a few hundred,flexible for the right hobby].

Physical limitations or preferences: [PHYSICAL?]

I prefer [SOCIAL:select:solo activities,group activities,a mix of both,starting solo and maybe joining others later].

Things I have enjoyed in the past: [PAST_INTERESTS?]

Things I have tried and did not enjoy: [DISLIKED?]

Living situation: [LIVING:select:apartment with limited space,house with space and yard,access to outdoor areas,urban with lots of nearby options,rural with fewer options].

Suggest 7-10 hobby ideas that match my preferences. For each, explain what it involves, why it fits my criteria, estimated cost to get started, time commitment needed, how to begin, and what I might like or dislike about it. Include a mix of popular hobbies and unexpected ones. Suggest which one to try first based on my constraints. Include tips for sticking with a new hobby past the initial excitement.
