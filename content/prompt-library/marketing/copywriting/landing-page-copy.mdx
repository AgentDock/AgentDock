# Landing Page Copy Writer

I need persuasive landing page copy that converts visitors into customers or leads.

The product or service is [PRODUCT_SERVICE].

The primary goal of this landing page is [GOAL:select:get email signups,sell a product,book a demo or call,start a free trial,download a resource,register for an event,get quote requests].

My target audience is [TARGET_AUDIENCE].

The main problem my offering solves: [PROBLEM]

The key benefits of my solution: [BENEFITS]

Key features to highlight: [FEATURES?]

Social proof I can include: [SOCIAL_PROOF?] (such as testimonials, customer count, reviews, logos, etc.)

The primary call to action button text: [CTA_BUTTON]

Any urgency or scarcity elements: [URGENCY?] (such as limited time, limited spots, deadline, etc.)

Common objections visitors might have: [OBJECTIONS?]

The tone should be [TONE:select:professional and credible,friendly and approachable,bold and confident,luxurious and premium,playful and engaging].

Write complete landing page copy including: a compelling headline and subheadline, a hero section that hooks attention, a problem and solution section, a benefits section with clear value propositions, a features section if relevant, a social proof section, an FAQ section addressing objections, and a strong closing with the call to action. Structure the copy for scanning with clear sections and make each section work to move the visitor toward conversion.
