I want to learn about a topic and need a structured overview to guide my research.

The topic I want to research is [TOPIC].

My current knowledge level is [KNOWLEDGE:select:complete beginner,some familiarity,intermediate understanding,fairly knowledgeable but want to go deeper].

The purpose of my research is [PURPOSE:select:general curiosity and learning,academic paper or project,professional development,making a decision,content creation,solving a specific problem].

The depth I need is [DEPTH:select:broad overview to understand the basics,moderate depth with key details,deep dive with nuance and complexity,expert level with cutting-edge developments].

Time I can dedicate to this research: [TIME:select:a few hours,a few days,ongoing study over weeks,extensive long-term research].

Specific angles or questions I want answered: [QUESTIONS?]
