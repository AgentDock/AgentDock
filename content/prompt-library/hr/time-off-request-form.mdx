# Time Off Request Form

You are an HR operations specialist with 14 years of experience managing leave administration, PTO policies, and absence tracking across corporate, healthcare, and government organizations. You have designed time-off request workflows for companies with 20 to 5,000 employees, ensured compliance with FMLA, ADA, and state-specific leave laws, and reduced scheduling conflicts by 40 percent through structured request processes. You know that a well-designed time off request form protects both the employee's rights and the employer's operational continuity.

Create a professional time off request form for [COMPANY_NAME] that employees can fill out and submit to their manager or HR department.

The form is for [EMPLOYEE_NAME], Employee ID [EMPLOYEE_ID], who works in the [DEPARTMENT] department under the supervision of [MANAGER_NAME].

The type of leave being requested is [LEAVE_TYPE:select:Vacation/PTO,Sick Leave,Personal Day,Bereavement Leave,FMLA Leave,Jury Duty,Military Leave,Unpaid Leave,Floating Holiday,Maternity/Paternity Leave,Other]. If Other, specify here: [OTHER_LEAVE_DETAIL?]

The requested time off begins on [START_DATE] and ends on [END_DATE]. The total number of days or hours requested is [DURATION]. The request is for [DAY_TYPE:select:Full day(s),Partial day - morning only,Partial day - afternoon only,Specific hours]. If specific hours, indicate the time window: [SPECIFIC_HOURS?]

The employee's current PTO balance is [PTO_BALANCE?] and their accrual rate is [ACCRUAL_RATE?]. Include a balance summary section that shows the remaining balance after this request is approved.

Reason or notes for this request: [REASON?]. Note that depending on company policy and leave type, a reason may or may not be required. For FMLA, bereavement, jury duty, or military leave, include a reminder that supporting documentation may be needed.

Coverage plan during the absence: [COVERAGE_PLAN?]. If the employee has arranged for a colleague to handle their responsibilities, note that person's name and the tasks being covered.

The approval workflow for this organization is [APPROVAL_WORKFLOW:select:Direct manager approval only,Manager approval then HR review,Department head and HR dual approval,Self-service with automatic approval under 3 days,Custom workflow]. If custom: [CUSTOM_WORKFLOW?]

Generate the following sections in the form. (1) Form Header with company name, form title, date of submission, and a unique request reference number. (2) Employee Information Block with name, employee ID, department, job title, and manager name. (3) Leave Request Details with leave type, start and end dates, duration, full or partial day selection, and reason field. (4) PTO Balance Summary showing current balance, hours or days requested, and projected remaining balance after approval. (5) Coverage and Handoff Plan listing who will cover the employee's duties, key tasks that need attention, and any deadlines falling within the absence period. (6) Policy Reminders with a brief note on documentation requirements for the selected leave type, blackout period warnings if applicable, and minimum notice expectations. (7) Approval Section with signature lines and date fields for the employee, the direct manager, and HR if required by the approval workflow. Include checkboxes for Approved, Denied, and Approved with Modifications, along with a comments field for the approver. (8) Record Keeping Footer with a note that copies of the request form should be filed in the employee's personnel record and the department attendance log. Include a reminder that FMLA, ADA, and other medical or supporting documentation must be stored in a separate confidential medical file, not in the general personnel record. Department attendance logs should record only presence or absence and leave type codes, not medical details or sensitive leave reasons.

Format the form with clear section borders, bold labels, and enough white space for handwritten entries if the form will be printed. Keep the language professional and neutral.
