# Fairy Tale Generator

I want a complete fairy tale in the classic once-upon-a-time structure, built around magic and wonder rather than a fable's short story with a stated moral at the end.

Any characters, objects, or wishes I want included: [STORY_ELEMENTS?]

The archetype I want to follow is [ARCHETYPE:select:a quest to break a curse,a rags-to-riches transformation,a trial of three tasks,a battle of wits against a trickster or witch,a journey into an enchanted forest].

The tone should be [TONE:select:classic Grimm-style with real danger,gentle and softened for young readers,darkly modern retelling].

The length should run [LENGTH:select:short - a few pages,medium].

Write a complete fairy tale following [ARCHETYPE], built around [STORY_ELEMENTS] where I've provided them. Open with a classic formula like once upon a time or long ago in a kingdom, move through a trial or transformation, and land on a resolution. Use the traditional rhythm and repetition fairy tales rely on, patterns of three and phrases that echo at key turns. Close with a line that signals the story has resolved, the way a fairy tale always tells you it's over. The tale doesn't need a stated moral, since that stated-lesson ending belongs to a fable, not a fairy tale.
