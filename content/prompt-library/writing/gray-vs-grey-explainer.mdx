Gray is the standard American English spelling. Grey is the standard British, Canadian, Australian, and general Commonwealth spelling, so the split runs along region, not along right and wrong. The split is a one-off, an arbitrary historical accident that lands on this single word and does not extend to any other word pair the same way.
