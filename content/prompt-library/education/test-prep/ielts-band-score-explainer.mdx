IELTS scores on a 9-band scale in half-band steps, from 0, did not attempt, to 9, expert user. The overall band is the average of the four skill bands, Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking, rounded to the nearest half band. An average ending in .25 rounds up to the next half band, and an average ending in .75 rounds up to the next whole band, so two candidates with the same raw average can land on different overall scores depending on exactly where that average falls.
