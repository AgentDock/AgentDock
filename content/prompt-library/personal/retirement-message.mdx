# Retirement Message

Write a heartfelt retirement congratulations message for [RETIREE_NAME].

My relationship to them is [RELATIONSHIP:select:colleague,direct report,manager,mentor,executive leader,longtime friend,family member,client,team member,business partner].

Their notable career achievements and contributions include [ACHIEVEMENTS].

The tone should be [TONE:select:warm and heartfelt,professional and respectful,lighthearted with humor,nostalgic and sentimental,inspiring and motivational,casual and friendly].

A favorite memory or quality I want to highlight is [MEMORY_OR_QUALITY?].

Create a message that:

1. Opens with a genuine acknowledgment of their career milestone and what their retirement means
2. Highlights their specific achievements and the impact they made on colleagues, the organization, or their field
3. Includes a personal touch that reflects our relationship and shared experiences
4. Expresses gratitude for their contributions, guidance, or friendship
5. Closes with warm wishes for their next chapter and what lies ahead

Keep the message sincere and avoid generic phrases. Make it feel personal enough that the recipient knows this was written specifically for them. The message should be suitable for a retirement card, email, or spoken tribute at a farewell gathering.
