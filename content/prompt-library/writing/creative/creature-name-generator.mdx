# Creature Name Generator

I need names for a creature or a monster species appearing in a story, campaign, or bestiary I'm building. This tool names beasts and species, not a full civilization or race of sentient people.

The creature is a [CREATURE_TYPE:select:fearsome predator or monster,magical beast or familiar,giant or colossal beast,small or mischievous creature,undead or eldritch horror,hybrid or chimera].

The setting is [SETTING:select:high fantasy,science fiction,folklore-inspired,horror].

Any detail worth building the name around: [CONTEXT?] (a habitat, a specific ability, a role it plays in the story)

Generate [COUNT:number:3-15] creature or species names based on these inputs. For each one, add a short note on what the name evokes, danger, size, habitat, or the specific dread or wonder it's meant to trigger. Vary the sound and length across the batch so a colossal beast doesn't share a naming pattern with a small mischievous one unless that's intentional. Match the tone of the setting closely, a horror creature name should not sound whimsical. Close with your top 3 picks and why they work.
