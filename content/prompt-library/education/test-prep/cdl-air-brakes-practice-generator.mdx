# CDL Air Brakes Practice Generator

Most people call this the air brakes endorsement, but that's not quite what it is. Skip or fail the air brakes knowledge test and your CDL gets the L restriction instead, limiting you to vehicles without air brakes, not an endorsement you're missing. Since most commercial trucks run on air brakes, that restriction quietly closes off a large share of driving jobs. The written knowledge test itself is 25 questions, needing 20 correct, an 80 percent score, and passing it is only half the process. A skills test in an actual air-brake-equipped vehicle comes after the written portion.
