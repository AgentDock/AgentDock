# Cruise Planner

I want to plan a cruise vacation and need help making smart choices at every stage, from picking the right ship to organizing my days on board and in port.

My cruise destination or region is [CRUISE_REGION:select:Caribbean,Mediterranean,Alaska,Northern Europe and Scandinavia,Asia and Pacific,South America,Transatlantic,Hawaii,Antarctica and Expedition].

I am traveling [TRAVEL_PARTY:select:solo,as a couple,with family including kids,with a group of friends,as a multigenerational family].

My trip length preference is [TRIP_LENGTH:select:short getaway (3-5 nights),week-long cruise (6-8 nights),extended voyage (9-14 nights),long cruise (15 plus nights)].

My overall budget range is [BUDGET:select:value-focused keeping costs low,moderate with room for some upgrades,premium willing to pay for quality,luxury where cost is not the main concern].

My top priorities for this cruise are [PRIORITIES] (such as relaxation, adventure excursions, fine dining, family entertainment, cultural exploration, nightlife, wellness and spa, photography opportunities).

My cabin preference is [CABIN_TYPE:select:inside cabin for the best rate,ocean-view cabin with a window,balcony cabin for private outdoor space,suite or premium cabin for extra room and perks].

Any dietary restrictions or accessibility needs: [SPECIAL_NEEDS?]

Previous cruise experience: [EXPERIENCE:select:first-time cruiser,have taken 1-2 cruises before,experienced cruiser with 3 plus sailings]

Based on my preferences above, create a complete cruise plan that covers the following.

Ship and cruise line recommendations. Suggest 2-3 specific cruise lines and ships that fit my region, budget, party type, and priorities. Explain why each one works for my situation and note any standout features like specialty restaurants, water parks, or adults-only areas.

Cabin selection guidance. Based on my cabin type preference and travel party, recommend the best deck location and cabin category. Include tips on which side of the ship offers better views for my route and whether upgrades make sense at my budget level.

Port excursion ideas. For each major port of call on a typical itinerary in my chosen region, suggest 2-3 excursion options ranging from active adventures to relaxed cultural experiences. Budget breakdown. Estimate costs beyond the base fare, including gratuities, drink packages, Wi-Fi, shore excursions, and specialty dining. Suggest where to save and where to spend based on my budget level. Include tips on booking timing and loyalty program benefits for repeat cruisers.
